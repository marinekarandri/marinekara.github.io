PythonPower BISQL

Problem Statement

Analyzing ~45K transactions where delivery times were inconsistent due to weather, traffic, and vehicles, causing SLA planning unreliability and customer rating drops.

Telco Customer Churn Prediction

PythonScikit-LearnSMOTE

Problem Statement

Addressing a high 26.5% churn rate among ~7,000 customers. Model needed high recall to ensure departing customers can be targeted for retention campaigns.

Methodology

  • Applied StandardScaler + SMOTE to resolve target class imbalance without leakage.
  • Evaluated RandomForest vs. XGBoost, LogisticRegression, DecisionTree.
  • Random Forest was chosen as it reached the highest Recall (72%) and AUC (83.4%).

Impact

The model catches 72% of actual churners. Fiber optic users, M2M contractors, and new customers were identified as highest risk, optimizing the retention budget significantly.

RAG Document QA Chatbot

LLMs & GenAILangChainFAISSFastAPI

Problem Statement

Customer support and new employees spent ~4 hours/day searching for information across 200+ internal SOPs and policy documents, causing severe operational delays.

Architecture & Approach

  • Parsed PDF/DOCX files (PyMuPDF) and chunked with RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter (500 tokens, 50 overlap).
  • Generated embeddings using OpenAI text-embedding-3-small, stored via FAISS vector database.
  • Implemented Maximal Marginal Relevance (MMR) retrieval for optimal semantic diverse results fed to GPT-4 context window.
  • Deployed a scalable FastAPI backend communicating with a Streamlit chat UI using Docker.

Result & Impact

Achieved 87.3% Answer Relevancy (RAGAS framework) and < 2.5s response time. Reduced search time from 4 hours down to 15 mins/day, handling 40% more support volume efficiently.
